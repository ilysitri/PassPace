Email Support Report an Issue

You can contact support by email or open an issue on GitHub. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Quick checks

  • Location permission is only needed when recording a run attempt.
  • Profile, optional photo, selected standards, and attempts are stored locally on your device.
  • If something looks wrong, try closing and reopening the app, then confirm your selected standard in the profile drawer.

Privacy

Pass Pace is designed around local storage. The app does not currently require an account, does not use advertising trackers, and does not sell personal data.

When you start a run attempt, Pass Pace uses device location to estimate distance, pace, and progress toward the selected benchmark. Location is used for the active run attempt experience.

Important disclaimer

Pass Pace is unofficial and is not affiliated with any military, police, fire, emergency, or government organisation. Standards are provided for personal benchmarking only. Always verify current requirements with the relevant organisation before relying on any benchmark.

Pass Pace is not medical advice. Consider your health, training history, and local conditions before attempting demanding physical activity.
